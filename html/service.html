Services

Peacock Ayurveda Garden is a relaxation sanctuary for guests who want to discover the benefits of Ayurveda medicine. Our treatments and procedures are inspired by the ancient and highly efficient Ayurvedic therapy Panchakarma, a therapy centered on body cleansing, as well as freeing the body and soul from harmful impulses. We also offer the perfect setting for yoga and meditation, and have specialists who prepare ayurvedic diets to fully enjoy the benefits of Ayurvedic healing. Our doctor is also a certified Master in acupuncture so additionally we provide acupuncture treatment when it is necessary. The procedure is not included into the service package and is additionally charged.

Ancient Ayurvedic treatments for modern illnesses

Since each body is different, we focus on providing personalized Ayurvedic treatment and procedures that meet the individual’s physical and mental needs. At your arrival in our Ayurvedic sanctuary, you will be consulted by a doctor specialized in Ayurveda medicine. Our doctor will assess your physical and mental condition and prescribe an individual treatment. Our main goal is to help you improve your health, restore your physical and mental strength, and ensure long lasting healing effects. The guests of Peacock Ayurveda Garden will have the unique opportunity to discover the healing powers of ancient natural treatments. Our treatments and procedures combine the use of herbs and plants with various types of Ayurvedic massages, yoga and meditation, an ayurvedic diet that balances the doshas of each person, as well as one of the most important forces of Ayurveda medicine – Panchakarma therapy. Ayurveda successfully treats or helps to relieve symptoms of illnesses such as asthma, obesity, migraine, skin diseases, spinal problems, paralysis, respiratory system diseases and other physical problems where traditional Western medicine sometimes fails. Our procedures also help people fight stress and mental tension.
